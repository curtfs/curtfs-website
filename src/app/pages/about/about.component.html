Who are we?

We are a group of Cairo University students participating in Formula Student competitions all over the world, having shared thoughts, beliefs and specific targets that we can make a difference in industry generally, and in Motorsports car field specially for the sake of a better future. Our journey started back in 2010 as a graduation project and ever since 2012 we have taken part in six different competitions, visiting three different countries (UK, Germany and Italy).

What is the Formula Student Competition?

Formula Student (FS) is Europe's most established educational engineering competition and is organized by IMechE and SAE. It has been running in the UK since 1998. Each team is tasked to produce a prototype car and present it to a hypothetical manufacturing firm. During the competition the teams must demonstrate the logic behind their proposal and must be able to demonstrate that in a series of STATIC & DYNAMIC events.
